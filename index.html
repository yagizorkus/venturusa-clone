Frequently asked questions

How do ı generate a report? ↓
An account is needed to generate a report. After logging in, you'll be redirected to the "New Report" page. Fill in the prompt and click on the magnifying glass icon to start generating a report.
Where can I view previously generated reports? ↓
To view previously generated reports, simply go to the "My Reports" page and all of the reports you have generated will be available for viewing.
How do I share my reports?↓
To share a report, you can make it public by clicking on the report name from the "My Reports" page. Once the report has been opened, you will see an option at the top to set the report to public. Simply click the option to set the report public, then copy the URL and send it to whoever you want to share the report with.
How do I subscribe to a paid plan?↓
To subscribe to a paid plan, you first need to register for an account on our platform. After registration, navigate to the pricing page, where you'll see the different plans available. At the bottom of each plan, you'll find an option to subscribe. Simply click on that option and follow the subsequent steps to complete your subscription.
How can I purchase an Advanced Report, regardless of my subscription status?↓
To purchase an Advanced Report with or without a subscription, follow these steps: Click on your profile image in the top right corner of the page. Navigate to the "Settings" section. Look for the "Get additional Advanced Reports" section. Choose the number of Advanced Reports you'd like to credit to your account. The price per Advanced Report will be automatically adjusted based on your current subscription plan. Complete the transaction, and your account will be credited with the selected number of Advanced Reports.
